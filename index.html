Curse of life

State: Prototype (Discontinued)

My Position: Game Designer / 3D Artist / additional programming 

 A top-down puzzle platformer with a voxel artstyle.

We play as elf-like treasure hunter who was given a death curse. Our life constantly drains and can only be regained at specific spots. Our goal is to find a most efficient path forwad before our health runs out.

The full scope of the game included switching between the death/life state to solve certain puzzles and additional abilities.

Habiter 

State: Finished

My Position: Game Designer / 2D Art 

A Puzzle-managment game made for Beginners Circle Game Jam. Made in Unity with a 5-person team.

We are a courier, also called a "habiter". Our job is to analize planets around us and then deliver creatures to them from many different near-extinct species. To do that we need to determine traits of both the planets and our creatures to then match them accordinly. For successful deliveries we are rewarded with many we can spend on upgrades.

The is a finished prototype with three levels.
